What is Palikkatakomo?

  • Palikkatakomo is a community and association in Finland which purpose is to develop LEGO hobby in Finland.
  • Forum was opened 2008 and association was formed 2012.

What does Palikkatakomo do?

  • We provide discussion platforms in our forums and in Discord.
  • We host inspirating building competitions and arrange large collaborative builds to LEGO events.
  • We keep active contact to LEGO Group and other LEGO hobbyist clubs

Who can join the community?

  • Anyone can join our forum, you do not need to be a member of association and there is no age limit
  • Any LEGO hobbyist over 15 years old can join Palikkatakomo association

Why to join?

  • You meet inspiring, supporting and knowledgeable people to discuss all thing LEGO.
  • You can participate our large collaborative build projects in events
  • You can store your LEGO pictures to our photo gallery
  • You support the LEGO hobbyist scene in Finland
